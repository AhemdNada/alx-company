Other Support Facilities Included

Solid Wax Pastilles Unit

Design capacity: 48 ton/day. Raw material: Hydro treated micro wax

  • Oil content wt: max 0.5 - 1%
  • Melting point: min 78 °C
  • Flash point (closed): min 200 °C
  • Viscosity @ 100 °C: 12 cSt
  • Colour: 1.5
  • Penetration: 11 mm

Finished product: solid wax pastilles ~3 mm diameter. Polyethylene bags of 15 kg.

Hydrogen Unit

This unit produces Hydrogen from Natural gas. Used for oil, wax, hexane and kerosene hydrotreating.

H2 Vol%: 99

CH4 Vol%: max 0.1

CO+CO2 Vol (ppm): max 20

Nitrogen Producing Unit

Two units of Nitrogen in lube oil complex

  • Unit "A" Capacity 200 Nm3/h
  • Unit "B" Capacity 600 Nm3/h
  • Nitrogen shipped to other companies by special car in Alex Petroleum
Wax Molding Unit

Design capacity: 38 ton/day. Raw material specs:

  • Oil content wt: max 0.5 - 1%
  • Melting point: min 78 °C
  • Flash point (closed): min 200 °C
  • Viscosity @ 100 °C: 12 cSt
  • Colour: 1.5
  • Penetration: 11 mm

Finished product: hard wax plates (46×30×4 cm, 4 kg). Packed 5 plates per 20 kg carton.
