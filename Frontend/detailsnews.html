Modernization Project Update

Enhancing efficiency, safety, and environmental performance

Our ongoing modernization program continues to deliver measurable gains across units. Through targeted upgrades, advanced analytics, and optimized workflows, we are improving throughput while maintaining rigorous safety and sustainability standards.

Key improvements include upgraded monitoring systems, enhanced recovery processes, and expanded preventive maintenance practices. These changes reduce unplanned downtime and improve product quality consistency.

In parallel, teams are focusing on training and knowledge sharing to embed best practices and ensure long-term operational excellence.

  • Advanced condition monitoring and predictive maintenance
  • Energy optimization across critical equipment
  • Enhanced emission control and reporting
  • Standardized procedures and safety drills

These initiatives reflect our commitment to continuous improvement and responsible operations that support the national economy.
