Unit Description

Established since 1997 and start production, with production capacity 100000 tons per year asphalt.

Bitumen types are:

  • Paving Asphalt 60/70
  • Hard Asphalt types: (6/12, 10/20, 20/30, 30/40, 40/50)
  • Oxidized Bitumen types: (15/115, 25/95, 25/85)
  • Semi-Oxidized Bitumen (80/100) - Recently Produced

These types are used in filling concrete intervals, insulating materials, cables manufacturing, and as adhesive materials in cooling rooms.

Now APC have new modified types for packing oxidised asphalt with different shapes like carton boxes & plastic bags with different sizes according to the desire of the client. Local & international market welcome these types because of its easy handling with new shapes and the same high quality specifications.
